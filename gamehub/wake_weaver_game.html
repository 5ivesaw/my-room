Wake Weaver

Turn every shot into temporary architecture. Build wake walls, split enemy paths, and detonate your own geometry before the tide eats the arena.

Core rule

Your darts leave foam-wake segments behind them. Those segments are solid to enemies and shots until they decay. Use them to split charges, trap pursuers, and carve escape lanes.

Danger rhythm

Enemies arrive in waves. Each wave increases tide pressure. When the tide rises, your wake decays faster, but shells and score bonuses rise too.

Controls

WASD move, mouse aim, left click fire, right click detonate the active dart, space dash, P pause. Between waves, spend shells on upgrades.

Objective

Survive the full sequence, then face the Leviathan: a boss that tears holes through your architecture and forces you to improvise under pressure.
